## Inuit organizations and government 

1. **Inuit Tapiriit Kanatami (ITK) — national Inuit representational organization**
   - About Canadian Inuit: https://itk.ca/about-canadian-inuit/
   - Main site: https://itk.ca/
   - Used for: Inuit Nunangat overview, regional populations, governance, cultural framing, national political history.

2. **Government of Canada — Crown-Indigenous Relations and Northern Affairs Canada**
   - Inuit overview: https://www.rcaanc-cirnac.gc.ca/eng/1100100014187/1534785248701
   - Used for: language and writing systems, tuberculosis history.

3. **Statistics Canada**
   - Languages in Nunavut, 2021: https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/n1/pub/89-657-x/89-657-x2024007-eng.htm
   - Indigenous language families — Inuktut: https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/n1/pub/41-20-0002/412000022025004-eng.htm
   - Used for: speaker populations, regional breakdown of Inuktut varieties, 2021 Census data.

4. **Government of Nunavut**
   - Inuit Qaujimajatuqangit principles: https://www.gov.nu.ca/en/culture-and-heritage/inuit-qaujimajatuqangit
   - Used for: eight IQ principles, governance integration.
